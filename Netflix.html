THE VAMPIRE DIARES

SEASON 1

 EPS 1.PILOT

Reeling from the death of her parents, 17-year-old Elena becomes interested in a handsome new student named Stefan, who's hiding a dark secret.

EPS 2.The Night of the Comet

Elena goes to the Salvatore house to talk to Stefan but finds Damon there instead. Damon reveals surprising information about Stefan's past.

EPS 3.Friday Night Bites

Elena invites Stefan and Bonnie to dinner, hoping the two will bond, but the evening is disrupted by the unexpected arrival of Damon and Caroline.

EPS 4.Family Ties

Stefan escorts Elena to the town's Founder's Party. Damon tells Elena a story about the Salvatores, leaving her with questions Stefan won't answer.

SEASON 2

 EPS 1.The Return

Elena arrives home to a nightmare. Damon is the first to realize that Katherine has returned.

EPS 2.Brave New World

When a confused and desperate Caroline leaves the hospital and joins her friends at the Mystic Falls Carnival, Damon wants to take immediate action.

EPS 3.Bad Moon Rising

Elena, Damon and Alaric go to Duke University to search through Isobel's research, hoping to uncover any clues surrounding the Lockwood family.

EPS 4.Memory Lane

Stefan is shocked when Katherine reveals new secrets about what really happened in 1864. Damon tries a new tactic to resolve his issues with Mason.

EPS 5.Kill or Be Killed

Tyler learns more about the Lockwood family curse from Mason, and Sheriff Forbes receives startling information.

SEASON 3

 EPS 1.The Birthday

It's Elena's 18th birthday, and as she searches for Stefan, Damon tries to keep her from doing anything that might draw Klaus's attention.

EPS 2. The Hybrid

Damon and Alaric reluctantly go along with Elena's new strategy to find Stefan, leading Damon into a dangerous fight with an unexpected enemy.

EPS 3.The End of the Affair

Stefan reunites with a vampire from decades earlier. In a flashback to the 1920s, he comes face-to-face with a shocking chapter from his wild past.

EPS 4.Disturbing Behavior

A frustrating new enemy pushes Damon over the edge, and his outburst has lasting consequences. Bonnie's strange encounter affects everyone.

EPS 5. The Reckoning
